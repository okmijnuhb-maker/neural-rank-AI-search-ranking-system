What is SHAP?

SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) explains the output of machine learning models. For each document ranking decision, SHAP tells you exactly how much each feature pushed the score up or down — making the AI completely transparent.

Key SHAP Insights

overlap is the most powerful feature
When query terms appear in the document, the ranking score increases significantly. High overlap ratio = strong positive SHAP value.
first_pos captures position importance
Documents where query terms appear early rank higher. Lower first_pos value = higher SHAP contribution to ranking score.
LSA adds semantic understanding
Even when exact words don't match, LSA cosine similarity captures semantic meaning. This is what makes LambdaMART smarter than BM25.
Feature interactions matter
BM25 × overlap interaction is the strongest pair — when both are high, the ranking score jumps significantly beyond their individual contributions.
